Join Our Team at Lapeer Aviation!

At Lapeer Aviation Inc., we’re always looking for passionate and skilled individuals to join our growing team. Whether you're an experienced aviation professional or just starting your career in the industry, we offer exciting opportunities in aircraft maintenance, avionics, and flight training.

Why Work With Us?

  • Aviation Excellence – Be part of a company dedicated to providing top-tier aircraft maintenance, avionics services, and flight training.

  • Hands-On Experience – Work with cutting-edge Garmin avionics, advanced aircraft systems, and custom-built aviation solutions.

  • Team-Oriented Environment – Join a group of aviation professionals who share a passion for keeping aircraft flying safely and efficiently.

  • Growth and Development – We encourage continuous learning and provide opportunities to expand your skills in a fast-paced aviation setting.

  • Competitive Compensation – We offer fair pay, benefits, and a workplace that values your expertise.

Interested in Joining Us?

We are always interested in hearing from talented A&P mechanics, avionics technicians, and flight instructors. If you’re interested in a career with Lapeer Aviation, we’d love to connect!

Send your resume and a brief introduction to gene@lapeeraviation.com, or give us a call at 810-664-6966 to learn more about available opportunities.
