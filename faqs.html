Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

General Questions

Q: Where is Lapeer Aviation located?

A: Lapeer Aviation is based at Flint-Bishop Airport (KFNT) in Flint, MI, offering aircraft maintenance, avionics, flight training, and custom panel fabrication.

Q: What services do you offer?

A: We specialize in aircraft maintenance, avionics installation and repair (Garmin-focused), custom panel fabrication, and flight training.

Q: Do you offer flight training?

A: Yes! We provide flight training with certified instructors and offer aircraft rentals for training and personal use.

Avionics and Maintence Questions

Q: What avionics brands do you install?

We are a Garmin-authorized installer and provide upgrades, installations, and repairs for Garmin avionics systems.

Q: Can you customize my aircraft panel?

A: Yes! We offer custom in-house panel fabrication for avionics upgrades and complete cockpit overhauls.

Q: Do you perform annual inspections and aircraft maintenance?

A: Absolutely. We handle annual inspections, 100-hour inspections, pre-purchase evaluations, and routine aircraft maintenance.
