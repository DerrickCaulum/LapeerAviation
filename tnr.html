Experience the Freedom of Flight with Lapeer Aviation!

Whether you're starting your journey as a pilot or need a reliable aircraft rental, Lapeer Aviation Inc. provides expert flight training and high-quality aircraft for rent. Based at Flint-Bishop Airport, we are committed to helping you achieve your aviation goals with top-tier instruction and well-maintained planes.

Flight Training – Build Skills with Expert Instruction!

Our certified flight instructors (CFIs) provide structured, hands-on training designed to help you become a confident and capable pilot. Throughout your training, you will:

  • Master cockpit operations and essential aircraft controls.

  • Develop skills to read and interpret flight instruments.

  • Train in real-world conditions, including various weather scenarios.

With a focus on safety and precision, our flight training programs are designed to help you succeed.

Start your aviation journey today. Contact us to learn more about our training programs.
