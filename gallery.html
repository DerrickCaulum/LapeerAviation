Custom-Made and In-House-Designed Panels

At Lapeer Aviation Inc., we take pride in offering expertly crafted, custom-made panels designed and built in-house to meet the specific needs of aircraft owners and operators. Our dedication to precision and innovation ensures that every panel we create is tailored to enhance functionality, safety, and aesthetics.

Why Choose Our Panels?

  • Tailored Designs: Every panel is customized to suit your aircraft’s unique specifications and operational requirements.

  • Quality Craftsmanship: Built by our skilled technicians using the latest tools and techniques, our panels deliver exceptional durability and performance.

  • Modern Technology: Whether you're upgrading your avionics or creating a cleaner, more intuitive cockpit layout, our panels are designed with cutting-edge technology in mind.

  • Streamlined Integration: Our in-house design process ensures seamless integration with your aircraft’s existing systems.

With our custom panel solutions, we strive to provide the perfect blend of functionality and aesthetics, helping pilots enjoy a more efficient and comfortable flight experience.
