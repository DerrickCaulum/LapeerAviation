Keep Your Aircraft in Top Condition!

At Lapeer Aviation Inc., we provide expert aircraft maintenance and repair services at Flint-Bishop Airport. Our experienced team is committed to keeping your aircraft airworthy, safe, and performing at its best.

Our Maintenance and Repair Services

We offer a full range of aircraft maintenance services, including:

  • Annual Inspections – Ensure your aircraft meets all regulatory and safety standards.

  • 100-Hour Inspections – Stay compliant and keep your aircraft in peak operating condition.

  • Pre-Purchase Inspections – Get a thorough evaluation before making an investment.

  • Aircraft Weighing – Maintain accurate weight and balance for optimal flight performance.
